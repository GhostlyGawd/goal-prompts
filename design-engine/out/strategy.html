№1

The Arcade, six ways

every variant runs: icon → favicon →
nav → 16px tab → one color
V1Stroke trio

Refined proportions of the original: two drawn arches, orange doorway. Honest ink-line feel; lighter presence at a distance.

V2Solid bandsRECOMMENDED

The board's flat-gouache technique: deep cobalt, light cobalt, cream, orange door — reads like a screen print. Strongest 16px presence; the favicon needs no tile because the mark brings its own.

V3Arch silhouette

The mark IS an arch (no square behind it) — cobalt, cream, orange nested. Distinctive silhouette in a tab row; slightly less detail at large sizes.

V4A-monogram

The arch crossed to read as a letter A — Auditorium's initial with the lamp dot beneath. Clever, but the crossbar fights the doorway story at small sizes.

V5Lamp in the archway

The bubble lamp hanging inside the arch — both board motifs in one. Rich at big sizes; at 16px the lamp reads as a blob.

V6Colonnade

Three arches in a row — literally an arcade, the middle door glowing. Wide, architectural; thin at favicon scale.
