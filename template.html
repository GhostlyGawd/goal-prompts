An MCP server with list_briefs, suggest_briefs, and get_brief — your agent pulls the right audit mid-conversation. Every brief also lives at a stable URL (/raw/30.md), each playbook ships a one-paste conductor, the machine index is /catalog.json, and real output lives in the sample reports.

Act on the reports

Your audits leave reports at the repo root — the Report Studio turns them into a checklist. Drop the .md files, tick the findings that matter, and one tap builds a targeted 47 · Fixer prompt containing exactly those findings. Client-side only; nothing leaves your browser. Prefer sequencing audits instead? Tap + seq on any cards to compose your own conductor.
