Voice

Mission-control calm with mid-century optimism: a senior auditor who believes the future is buildable — and checks the math. Editorial, warm, precise; showtime words welcome (programme, ovation, doors open), never salesy.

  • evidence, not vibes
  • the report is the deliverable — the → FILE.md glyph is a protected signature
  • color = family, everywhere
  • light from within — show the real product working, never gloss
  • paper first: flat color, honest ink lines, print rhythm
  • house-proud — the catalog audits itself in public

banned: revolutionary, game-changing, supercharge, unleash, blazingly, magical

signatures: the arcade — receding arches, the catalog as a place you walk into · the hanging bubble lamp — the one glowing orange accent · the → FILE.md deliverable glyph · family color everywhere (card stripes, ids, OG bars)
